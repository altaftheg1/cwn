What to contact us about

  • Corrections — If you spotted a factual error in a story, please let us know. We take accuracy seriously and will correct it quickly.
  • Feedback — Tell us what you like, what you don't, and what we should cover more of.
  • Source suggestions — Know a reliable UAE source we're not monitoring? We'd love to add it.
  • Community reports — For ground observations (traffic, weather, events), use the "On The Ground" section on the homepage.
  • Subscription issues — Not receiving your digest emails? Email us with the address you subscribed with.
  • General enquiries — Anything else — we're happy to chat.
