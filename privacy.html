Plain English summary CWN is a read-only news aggregator. We do not sell your data. We do not run ads. The only personal data we store is your email address (if you subscribe) and community posts (if you submit one). Everything else is processed anonymously.
01

Who We Are

Central Watch News (“CWN,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is an independent UAE news aggregation service. We summarise publicly available news from official UAE government sources and established newsrooms, rewriting articles in plain, calm English using AI.

CWN is operated as an independent project and is not affiliated with any UAE government body, news organisation, or media company.

02

What We Collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to operate the service:

Data type When collected Stored?
Email address When you subscribe to news digests Yes — on our server
Subscription preferences When you choose topics and digest times Yes — on our server
Community post content When you submit an “On The Ground” report Yes — in Supabase if approved
Display name (optional) If you choose not to be anonymous in a post Yes — shown publicly
Contact info (optional) If you provide it with a named post Yes — stored privately, never shown
IP address With every request to our server No — used only for rate-limiting, never logged
Language preference When you switch UI language No — stored locally in your browser only
Search queries When you use the search box No — filtering happens entirely in your browser
We do not collect We do not collect names, phone numbers, location data, browsing history, device identifiers, or any tracking information beyond what is listed above.
03

How We Use Your Information

Each piece of data we collect is used for exactly one purpose:

  • Email address — to send you the news digest emails you signed up for. Nothing else.
  • Subscription preferences — to ensure you receive only the topics and delivery times you chose.
  • Community post content — to display approved ground reports publicly on the CWN homepage and to moderate submissions via AI.
  • Optional contact info — held privately for verification purposes only. Never displayed or shared.
  • IP addresses — used momentarily to enforce a rate limit of 3 community posts per IP per hour. Not logged or stored.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, analytics tracking, or sale to third parties. We do not build user profiles.

04

AI Processing

CWN uses Claude, an AI model by Anthropic, for two purposes:

  • Article rewriting — publicly available news articles are sent to Claude to be rewritten in plain English. These articles contain no personal user data.
  • Community post moderation — when you submit an “On The Ground” report, its text content (not your contact info) is sent to Claude for moderation. Claude reviews it and returns an approval decision. The text is processed by Anthropic’s API under their Privacy Policy.
What is NOT sent to AI Your email address, contact information, display name, and IP address are never sent to any AI model.

Article translations use Google Translate, operated by Google LLC. Translated text is sent to Google’s free translation endpoint. No personally identifiable information is included. Please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy for details on how they handle translated content.

06

Data Storage & Security

Email addresses and subscriber preferences are stored in a JSON file on our server, which is not publicly accessible. Community posts (if approved) are stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database with Row-Level Security enabled. Only approved posts are publicly readable; contact information is protected and never exposed through any API.

We retain subscriber data for as long as you remain subscribed. You may unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link in any digest email. Community posts may be retained to maintain the integrity of the public feed. If you wish to have a post removed, contact us.

Retention summary Email subscriptions: until you unsubscribe.
Community posts: indefinite (contact us to request deletion).
Article cache: rolling cache, automatically replaced by newer content.
07

Cookies & Local Storage

CWN does not use cookies.

We use your browser’s localStorage to remember one preference: the UI language you have selected. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

Third-party services (Google Fonts, jsDelivr) may set their own cookies as part of standard CDN operation. We have no control over these.

08

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your data. For email subscriptions, simply unsubscribe. For community posts or contact information, email us.
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of all digest emails at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email.
  • Objection — object to any processing you did not consent to.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 14 days.

09

Children

CWN is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Effective” date at the top of this page. Continued use of CWN after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

If we make a material change that affects how we use subscriber email addresses, we will notify subscribers directly by email before the change takes effect.

Questions about your privacy?

Reach out and we’ll respond within 14 days. Include “Privacy” in your subject line.
