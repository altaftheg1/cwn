About DUB

Calm news for UAE residents

No panic. No agenda. Just clear summaries from trusted UAE sources.

Our mission

TheDubaiBrief collects key UAE updates and rewrites headlines and summaries in calm, plain language.

The goal is simple: make important information easier to understand for residents and expats.

This is an independent student project based in Dubai.

What we stand for

No panic

Alarmist wording is reduced so the focus stays on facts and decisions.

No agenda

No paid political spin and no sponsored narrative in the summaries.

Just facts

Every item traces back to a source article you can verify directly.

How it works

1. Pull stories from official and established UAE publishers.

2. Normalize and deduplicate the feed.

3. Rewrite titles and summaries to remove sensational language.

4. Publish the calm feed with links to original reporting.
