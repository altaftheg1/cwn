Content & Sources

All news content displayed on CWN is paraphrased and summarised from publicly available articles and announcements published by established UAE news sources, including:

  • WAM — Emirates News Agency (wam.ae) — official UAE state news agency
  • Gulf News — gulfnews.com — UAE’s largest English-language newspaper
  • Khaleej Times — khaleejtimes.com — English-language UAE daily
  • Emirates 24|7 — emirates247.com — UAE English-language news portal
  • UAE Government Portal — u.ae — official UAE federal government portal

Every article displayed on CWN links directly to its original source. CWN does not claim ownership of any original reporting. Original sources are always credited and linked. CWN’s AI-generated plain English summaries are paraphrases, not reproductions of original text.

Original source always credited Every article shows its source name and includes a “Read Original Article” button linking to the full original piece on the source’s own website.
