🚌 Roads & Transport

🏫 Schools & Education

Spring break is in effect for most UAE government and private schools (approx. 1–22 March). Schools are expected to return to in-person learning from 23 March onwards. Check with your school directly for exact dates. Distance learning is not mandated.

🏠 Daily Life

Shops, malls, restaurants, and public spaces are open and operating normally. No restrictions on movement or gatherings are in effect. Residents can continue daily activities as usual.
